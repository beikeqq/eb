About Us

Everbridge Business Advisory Limited, established in Hong Kong, was founded by a professional team with strong backgrounds in technology, finance, and management. The team has accumulated extensive experience in multinational enterprises and entrepreneurial ventures, covering fields such as engineering equipment, information technology, financial management, and corporate operations. With interdisciplinary expertise and broad resource integration capabilities, the team provides clients with comprehensive and reliable support.

The company's business is carried out through corporate strategy consulting, project management consulting, software development consulting, and equipment procurement services, offering in-depth advisory and integrated solutions. Everbridge focuses on serving enterprise clients, undertaking cross-border business involving international investment, global projects, and international trade. By assisting clients in system development and strategic expansion, Everbridge is committed to becoming a trusted partner in global competition.
